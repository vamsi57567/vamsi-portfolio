02 — About

The analyst behind
the dashboards

I'm a Data Analyst and Power BI Developer with a sharp focus on business intelligence, reporting, and helping organisations make better decisions through clean data and clear visuals. Currently completing my Master of Professional Studies in Data Science at the University of Maryland, Baltimore County.

At Minnodi LLC, I design Power BI dashboards that give leadership real answers — tracking KPIs, revenue performance, and operational health across the business. I write DAX, build data models, automate reporting with Power Query and Excel VBA, and work directly with stakeholders to turn business questions into dashboards people actually open every morning.

I have also published peer reviewed research in IEEE on detecting deforestation from satellite imagery using machine learning. Because good analysis does not stop at the business — it can go anywhere data matters.

05 — Research

Peer-reviewed
publication

IEEE Xplore  ·  Conference Publication  ·  2024
Deforestation Detection from Remote Sensing Images using Machine Learning
Vamsi Krishna Peeta et al.  ·  SRM Institute of Science and Technology
IEEE Xplore  ·  DOI: 10.1109/10724972  ·  Conference Paper

Published in IEEE Xplore, this research applies machine learning and deep learning to satellite remote sensing data to analyze and detect deforestation patterns at scale. The study extracts key parameters including vegetation cover change, forest loss, and land use dynamics — combining CNNs with remote sensing to produce deforestation maps suitable for real environmental monitoring.
