Machine Learning · IEEE Published Capstone
Deforestation Detection from Satellite Imagery
Developed and trained a custom convolutional neural network to detect deforested regions from satellite remote sensing imagery — extracting vegetation cover change, forest loss rates, and land use dynamics at scale. The model processes multispectral satellite bands, applies spatial feature extraction, and outputs pixel-level deforestation probability maps. This work went beyond a course project — it was rigorous enough to be accepted and published as a conference paper in IEEE Xplore.
